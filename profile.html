Our story

Penceli organisation was founded in the year 2019 by advocate Roney Mutuma who has a passion and talent for making paintings. He started as a simple t-shirt painter for fun and that is when his idea for starting his own art gala was formed. he used spraying cans to make designs on shirts and other than t-shirt panting he was good in drawing portraits and landscape. combining this two he was able to start the Penceli organization. Since then, he has been reaching out to artist with potential and not able to advertise or display their diverse types of artistry and give them a platform to display their art to the world. This is made possible for he organizes annual art galas where we display some of the paintings and artwork of the artist under our organization for selling and viewing to the public who then can purchase the painting. Penceli organization majors on a few art types like:
  1. Abstract
  2. Sculpture
  3. Realism
  4. Pop art
  5. Oil art
  6. Narrative art



Training:

we offer painting classes where experienced artists take people who have enrolled through the fundamentals of paintings. This is still a way of giving back to the community who have helped our organization through the years
